Check-in

Check-in is after 4:00 PM but should you desire to drop your bags, we can usually accommodate. Most days check in can be earlier, but you’ll need to ask and we can’t guarantee.

Check-out

Check-out is at 10:30 AM unless otherwise scheduled. There are no chores other than closing the door on your way out. (Unless you smoke and/or have pets, see those sections).

Trash Receptacle vs. Recycling Bin

The trash receptacle is black, the recycling bin is turquoise. Please recycle only bottles, cans, and clean paper/cardboard. All else goes in the black bin. (See notes for pet waste and cigarette butts in those sections).

Smoking

Absolutely no smoking in the suite. Smoking of any kind is allowed on the pool deck / patio or outside the screened area. If you smoke herb, remember that we can smell strong odors, so please open and close the container as rapidly as possible. When smoking on the patio, please close the room door. An ashtray is placed on the patio windowsill for your use, please bag your butts/ashes on your last morning with us and dispose of them in the black garbage bin.

Laundry

Our washer and dryer are available for your use. We offer scent free detergent, softener, and dryer sheets.

Parking

Please park on the left side of the driveway up to the cone, the cone lets you know you’ve reached the correct address and also prevents blocking either sidewalk.

Pool

We encourage you to use the pool as often as you like, we love it and use it 4 nights a week during summer and at least twice a week during winter. There is no fee for using the pool, however, please be safe and if you have young children, please be cautious. The tan cabinet just outside your door contains pool use supplies such as towels, hats, sunscreen, bottle openers, koozies, inflatables, etc. You may use anything in the cabinet.

Hot Tub

The hot tub is also available for your use, (ask us for the controller), and incurs a fuel fee depending on the temperature, (winter use is expensive), and how much you use it. We have an accurate app that tells us how many liters are used - fuel prices change with each fill so the cost is determined for each guest. As a guide only, winter use costs about $19 / hour, summer use is closer to $5 / hour. The system is programmed to stay on for two hours, so if it shuts down while you are still enjoying the tub, just press the “spa” button again. Once you get out, press the “pool” button. Payment is required in advance.

Walking

Our neighborhood is very safe and very walkable – enjoy if you wish. You will notice walkers, bikers, joggers etc. every day of your stay. Please don’t park in a manner that blocks the sidewalk.

Gardens

Ask about our local botanical gardens, we’ve been to them all.

Pets

Well behaved pets are welcome. Please collect pet waste and place the collection bags on the opposite side of the sidewalk from the black garbage can; on the day the city collects garbage, I will collect the bags and dispose of them.

Restaurants

There are literally hundreds of restaurants within 5 miles. Here are a few of our favorites that are less than 3 miles away. If you see a restaurant that you are interested in, ask us - we may have eaten there. On the other hand, restaurants are changing and opening rapidly and there are many we’ve not tried. Fine dining establishments are further away so are listed at the end.

We whole-heartedly recommend the following nearby establishments organized by distance from our house:


Braza’s Chicken - Peruvian and Latin American

1.5 Miles

4797 S. Orange Avenue, Orlando FL 32806

Every meal comes with three sauces, none of them overly spicy, but if you ask, you can get a very spicy sauce. When we leave, all of the sauces are usually gone, eaten with our french fries and/or our chicken. French fries come with a few frankfurters cut up about an inch long.

SoDough Square
Tin & Taco Sodo

1.5 Miles

419 E Michigan St, Orlando, FL 32806

Two restaurants sharing a dining room:

SoDough Square:serves authentic Detroit style pizza made fresh daily. Fabulous pizza.

Tin & Taco Sodo:serves trendy casual tacos and offers a wide selection of craft beer.

RockPit Brewing

1.6 Miles

10 W Illiana St, Orlando, FL 32806

Neighborhood brewery and distillery with a large family friendly taproom, (and really, we mean friendly - I stopped by before they opened and again on their closed day and both times they welcomed me in and provided a free beer of my choice), with a revolving number of small-batch beers. Adjacent to Cecil’s BBQ for a full night out. New beers are released every Thursday.

Cecil’s Texas Style BBQ

1.6 Miles

2800 S. Orange Ave, Orlando, FL 32806

You won’t leave Cecil’s disappointed or hungry! Even though I don’t drink soda, I order root beer here because they have self-serve soft-serve so I always make myself a root beer float.

Swirlery Wine Bar

1.7 Miles

1508 E Michigan St, Orlando, FL 32806

A small production wine shop and tasting room with Sommelier selected wines by the glass as well as excellent craft beer. Food is only available for special events.

Johnny's Other Side

1.8 Miles

1619 E Michigan St, Orlando, FL 32806

Beer, wine, and bourbon bar serving a large selection of quality meals. I love their fried green tomatoes!

Vanbarry's Public House

1.9 Miles

4120 S Orange Ave, Orlando, FL 32806

Vanbarry’s menu is eclectic, always good paired with a cocktail bar for excellent drinks. Last time we were there I asked for the bean soup that is usually a side and enjoyed it immensely. When you sit down, a jar of home-made pickles is delivered to your table. Yum yum!! Live music 7 days a week starting at 7:00 PM, brunch on Sundays.

À La Cart SoDo

2.4 Miles

2207 E Michigan St, Orlando, FL 32806

Outdoor dining location (with indoor seating) for at least 8 semi-permanent mobile food vendors offering a variety of food options with a large craft beer selection as well as wines, ciders, and seltzers.

Las Cazuelas Tienda Mexicana & Restaurant

2.7 Miles

4024 Conway Road, Orlando FL 32812

This hole-in-the-wall grocery and restaurant is a hidden gem servicing excellent Mexican food. This is our go-to Mexican Restaurant. No ambiance, but the food is great!

The Roque Pub

3.4 Miles

3076 Curry Ford Rd, Orlando, FL 32806

Craft beer bar with a pool table, darts, and a pet-friendly patio - my go-to beer place. Limited menu but excellent quality of food prepared on site when ordered.
